Crispy Ginger Beef

Description

Craving Chinese tonight? Well look no further than your own kitchen! This is one of the classics! Crispy wok fried beef with a lovely soy sauce and ginger dressing combined with onions, assorted bell peppers, garlic and carrots to create a succulent stir-fry! Serve with your choice of starch!

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 pound flank stead, cut into thin strips
  • 1/2 cup canola oil, or as needed
  • 1 large carrot, cut into matchstick-size pieces
  • 1 green bell pepper, cut into matchstick-size pieces
  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into matchstick-size pieces
  • 3 green onions, chopped
  • 1/4 cup minced fresh ginger root
  • 5 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
